Specialty coffee near you!

Have you ever asked yourself, where does the coffee you drink come from? You can have a taste of each part of the world in just a cup. How? Let's learn this together!

1. COLOMBIA - did you know that this country supplies 15% of the world's coffee? Also, they produce some high - quality Arabica. In My coffee shop you can taste some Supremo and Extra from Colombia!

2. GUATEMALA - If you want to taste an intense, tart flavor, we will prepare you some Antigua Volcanic from Guatemala. Its particular flavors come from the mountainous areas it grows in.

3. COSTA RICA - In search of a classic taste? Costa Rica, Margarita and Cashier varieties are ready to bring the classic back to you. These beans grow on the volcanic soils and generally they are wet - processed.

4. THE ARABIAN PENINSULA - Have a taste of the most famous coffees in the world - the Arabian Mocha - and you will fall in love forever! Why is it so special you may wander... it has a chocolaty flavor and slight wine notes. Due to very small production volumes, it is almost never exported, however, you will find some limited edition of it here!

5. ETHIOPIA - Also one of the countries with a large variety of high - quality coffee, we bring the Ethiopian taste to you through the Harrar variety.
